A real conversation

  I'm building your list now.
  Will you need concrete for posts?"

✓ List ready — 14 items, $847 est.

🤖 Understanding Context

Interprets "12×16 deck" → calculates board feet, joist spacing, beam sizing, and post count automatically.

🛒 Matching Store Inventory

Maps every item to this store's SKUs, confirms availability, and notes substitutions if something is out of stock.

❓ Asks the Right Questions

Proactively asks about posts, stairs, railing, and finish so nothing is forgotten before the customer leaves.
