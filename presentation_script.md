# AIMy — Presentation Script
**3 presenters · 6 slides · ~3 minutes total (~1 min each)**

---

## SPEAKER 1 — Intro & Problem
*Slides 1–2*

**[Slide 1]** We built **AIMy** — your in-store AI assistant. One button, your voice, and you get a done list. It’s in-store, voice-first, and there’s no app to download.

**[Slide 2]** Why did we build it? Because **stores are huge and help isn’t.** No one’s around when you need them. You leave with the wrong list, the wrong route, and you’re back tomorrow. One store manager put it like this: *“Customers come in knowing what they want to build — but leave unsure if they got everything they need.”* That’s the gap AIMy closes.

---

## SPEAKER 2 — Solution & How It Works
*Slides 3–4*

**[Slide 3]** So **this is AIMy.** It’s a voice AI shopping assistant at the store entrance. Customers press the **Easy Button**, describe what they need, and AIMy does the rest. You tell AIMy your project or question, you get a complete item list, an optimized store route, and an estimated subtotal upfront. One press — no app, no login, no screen. Just your voice and an instant answer.

**[Slide 4]** Under the hood, it’s **five steps to a perfect shopping trip.** One: customer presses the Easy Button at the door. Two: AIMy asks — voice conversation about what they need. Three: the AI thinks — Claude maps their needs to store inventory. Four: they get an item list — quantities, aisles, prices. Five: optimized route and estimated subtotal. You can say a project — *“I’m building a 12×16 deck”* — ask a question — *“What do I need to patch drywall?”* — or give a list. AIMy fills in the rest.

---

## SPEAKER 3 — What You Get & Close
*Slides 5–6*

**[Slide 5]** Every AIMy conversation gives you **three outputs from one conversation.** A **smart shopping list** — every item with exact quantities, aisle numbers, and prices, tailored to your project. An **optimized route** — one clean sweep, no backtracking; heavy items first, checkout last. And an **instant subtotal** — know your budget before you grab a single item. The payoff: **3× more items per trip**, **67% fewer return trips**, **20 minutes saved** per visit — and **zero apps** to download.

**[Slide 6]** So that’s AIMy. **Press the button. Describe your project. Walk out with everything you need.** One button at the door — AIMy is ready. What are you building? Thank you.
